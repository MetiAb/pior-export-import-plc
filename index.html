About Our Company

Pior Export Import PLC is an Ethiopian company dedicated to the distribution of high-quality agricultural and natural products. With deep roots in Ethiopia's rich agricultural heritage, we specialize in coffee distribution and natural salt sourcing, connecting local producers with trusted partners worldwide.

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and fair trade practices ensures that every product we distribute maintains the highest standards while supporting the communities that produce them.

Our Premium Coffee

We distribute premium Ethiopian coffee sourced from the Wollega region, known for its distinctive rich flavor, full body, and exceptional quality. Our coffee is carefully selected and processed to preserve its authentic Ethiopian character.

Supplied directly to distributors who value consistency and authenticity, our Wollega coffee beans are perfect for those who appreciate a cup with complex flavor notes and a smooth finish.

Our Natural Salt

Our natural salt is sourced from the Afar region, an area renowned for traditional salt harvesting methods that date back centuries. The salt is carefully hand-harvested and processed to maintain its purity and natural mineral composition.

Rich in essential minerals and free from artificial additives, Afar salt is prized by chefs and food producers for its unique texture and flavor profile that enhances any culinary creation.
